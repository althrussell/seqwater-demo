## 1. Problem statement

The Caboolture Pump Station (`PMP-014`) is showing a
sustained increase in failure probability score over the most recent
refresh windows. The asset is a single-string supply
asset for the Caboolture demand zone. A failure during
peak demand would create a continuity-of-supply risk indicator at
**Red**.

## 2. Options analysis

| Option | Description                                | Capital | Risk reduction |
|--------------------|---------------------------------------------------------|-------------------|--------------------------|
| Option 0           | Do nothing — continue preventive maintenance | $0.0M             | 0.00                      |
| Option 1           | Mechanical refurbishment, retain motors      | $1.4M             | 0.18                      |
| Option 2           | Full replacement, single-string equivalent   | $3.6M             | 0.41                      |
| Option 3           | Full replacement plus second-string addition | $5.9M             | 0.62                      |

The recommended option is **Option 3** — full
replacement plus a second-string addition. The risk
reduction score is the highest in the capital options register and
removes the single-string dependency on the Caboolture
zone.
