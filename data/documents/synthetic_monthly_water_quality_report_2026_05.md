## 3. Plant performance

### 3.1 North Pine WTP (`WTP-NORTHPINE`)

The North Pine WTP recorded an end-of-period Watch
indicator on the turbidity stream, sustained over the final 6 sampling
windows. Chlorine residual remained inside the operating
envelope. Recommended actions include increasing the
sampling frequency for the upstream raw water inlet and validating the
coagulation setpoints against the catchment quality
trend.

### 3.2 Landers Shute WTP (`WTP-LANDERSHUTE`)

The Landers Shute WTP recorded intermittent turbidity
excursions during a upstream storm cell window.
flocculation performance recovered within operational SLA.
Recommended actions include pre-emptive coagulant dose review
ahead of the upcoming forecast rainfall window.
